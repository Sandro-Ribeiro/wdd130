HISTORY

The passion for nature and adventure sports has always driven us. Our journey began more than ten years ago when a group of adventurous friends decided to explore the rapids and rivers of EUA, fueled by adrenaline and a love for the outdoors. Over time, we founded Rafting Dry OAR, to provide unforgettable and safe adventures on wild waters. We started small, but our passion for rafting quickly attracted adventurers of all ages and experience levels. Our team consists of certified and passionate guides who ensure that each expedition is the perfect blend of fun, safety, and respect for the environment. Come be part of this story and discover the thrill of riding the rapids with Rafting DRY AOR!
